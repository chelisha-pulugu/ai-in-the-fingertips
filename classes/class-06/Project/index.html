— About

Four people,
one quiet noise

Hollow Pine began as late-night recordings in a rain-soaked garage on the outskirts of Portland. Four friends — a poet, a painter, a drifter, and an architect — searching for a sound that felt like memory.

Today, their music drifts between folk and dream pop, layering intimate vocals over textured strings and ambient light. They make space for the listener to breathe.
