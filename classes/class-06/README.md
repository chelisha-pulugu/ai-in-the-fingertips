# Reflection Question

## Question
Go to the homepage of any AI product you have heard of — Notion, Linear, Vercel, or any startup. Look only at the hero section. What is the headline? What is the CTA? What emotion does the design create? Do you think it works? Why or why not?

## Answer
I looked at the homepage hero section of Notion AI.

### Headline
“Write. Plan. Organize. With AI at your side.”
### CTA
“Get Notion free”

### Emotion Created by the Design
The design creates a feeling of simplicity, productivity, and calmness. The clean layout, minimal colors, and organized spacing make the product feel easy to use rather than overwhelming. It gives the impression that work can become more structured and efficient with the help of AI.

### Does It Work?
Yes, I think it works very well because the hero section communicates the product’s value immediately. The headline is short and easy to understand, even for someone who has never used AI tools before. The CTA is also clear and low-pressure because it offers free access, encouraging users to try the product without hesitation.
The overall design matches the product’s purpose. Since Notion is meant for organization and productivity, the minimal and clean interface builds trust and makes the platform appear professional and user-friendly. Instead of trying to impress users with too many animations or technical details, it focuses on clarity and usability, which makes the hero section highly effective.
